about us

Berkeley High Ultimate Frisbee was founded in 2007 by a group of students. Since its inception, Berkeley High Coup has expanded into three seperate teams: the varsity team, Red, the jv team, Yellow, and most recently an all freshman boy's team called Lo Coup. In recent years, Red has fielded very successful teams, winning the state championship in 2013, 2014, and 2016. This year, Red took second at the Spaghetti Western tournament, in front of all other California teams.

One fundamental part of High Coup's team culture is to perform a Haiku for their opponents after every game. It is a demonstration of Ultimate Frisbee's value of Spirit of the Game, which stresses being an honorable opponent and playing fair. However, Spirit of the game is about more than just responsibility. It is also about having fun playing a sport that you love. This year High Coup was truly a family that loved eachother
